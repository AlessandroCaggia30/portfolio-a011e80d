Thesis

The Phillips Curve: Identification, Expectations, and the Role of Central Banks

BSc Thesis — Supervisor: Prof. Antonella Trigari, Bocconi University (2024)

A literature review and empirical investigation of the Phillips Curve, examining identification challenges in estimating the inflation-unemployment trade-off, the role of inflation expectations in shaping the curve's slope, and how central bank credibility affects the relationship. The thesis surveys the evolution from the original Phillips (1958) specification through the expectations-augmented framework to modern New Keynesian formulations.
